Gigiau - Your data and privacy

Thank you for contributing to Gigiau.

Gigiau is an aggregation website that shows lots of events that are happening in the local area. It automatically picks up event listings from venues' and promoters' websites and Facebook pages.

We obtain the agreement of the owners of the websites and Facebook pages before picking up their event data.

If you look at the events on Gigiau.uk

If you're just looking at the Gigiau website, we don't ask or keep any information about you at all. Your preference of language is kept on your own device, and not uploaded to our server.

We do count the frequency of access to our data, but we don't track the usage of individual users.

If you're an events promoter

If you manage a Facebook page

You can advertize events on the Events tab on your page. If you add your page to our poll list, Gigiau will then automatically look at your events list once every hour.

You can remove your page from our list at any time.

  • We'll keep your Facebook ID securely on our server.
  • We'll download your Facebook events and show them on our website, each with a link to your event page.

If you have a website with an events page

We can arrange, with your agreement, to pick up events from there.

If you don't have a website or Facebook page

Send a poster for your event to us at info@gigiau.uk

Contact us

Get in touch with us at info@gigiau.uk
